Relationship Discovered Between Healthcare and Poverty

The scatterplot above shows the relationship bewteen the lack of healthcare and poverty in US states.

Some interesting insights:

  • Overall, there is a positive relationship between the percentage of individuals that lack healthcare and those in poverty, i.e the higherthe poverty rate, the higher individuals lack healthcare.
  • Texas has the highest percentgae of individuals lacking healthcare, while Masschusetts has the lowest rates of poverty.
